MapleStory Mini Toolkit • Site Plan

Lightweight tools for KMS players

Site Name

MapleStory Mini Toolkit

This name communicates a compact, fast toolkit focused on everyday MapleStory calculations and lookups.

Optional domain idea: maple-mini.tools

Site Purpose

  • Provide a searchable item database with filters and quick detail views.
  • Offer calculators: stat and potential math, Star Force cost, and upgrade efficiency comparison.
  • Deliver concise beginner guides and a glossary.
  • Collect user feedback and item suggestions via an accessible form.

Scenarios

  1. How much will it cost to enhance a level 150 item from 10★ to 17★ with current meso rates?
  2. Which of two rings gives higher final stat after potential and flame lines?
  3. Where can I find a simple upgrade flow for a returning player?
  4. Can I save favorite items and revisit them later on my phone?
